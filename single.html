WaterWay Wish

A Collaborative Learning Board Game to Conserve Water

WaterWay Wish is a board game which aims to teach children about water consciousness. The project aims to target the water crisis issue and how collaborative efforts can lead to positive results. The objective was to connect people with water beyond their daily use of it as a commodity, trying to have a sustainable attitude towards water that can alleviate stress and anxiety some experience in their day-to-day life due to the water crisis and achieving these goals through play and interaction.

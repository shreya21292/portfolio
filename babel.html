Babel

An Interactive Card Game Based on Nature

'Babel' is an interactive card game, based on the environmental balance cycle and effect of human intervention on it. The initial brief of the project was to design a playful experience guided by visuals and sound inspired by nature. Its a 1-4 player game for ages above 8.
